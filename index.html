Farlo/Sports/Mainpage

Hockey

Farlo HC was the first team created from the Farlo Sports Organization. It was founded in 2015 and is still active year round. Farlo HC plays throughout the year during winter, spring, summer, and fall seasons. It is the only team from the Farlo organization that does this.

Farlo HC is captained by Manjinder Varya

Football

Farlo Football was created in 2021 and is the newest addition to the Farlo Sports Organization. In their inaugural season, they finished the regular season 7-1 and ultimately lost in the finals.

Farlo Football is captained by Gurvir Sohal

Softball

Farlo SC was created in 2020 and has enjoyed success since its inception. In their most recent season, they advanced to the finals, before ultimately losing.

Farlo SC is captained by Wajih Anwar

Soccer

Farlo FC was created in 2021 and enjoyed immediate success. In their unaugural season, they advanced to the finals in a penatly thriller. They were able to win the championship in their maiden season in another penalty-filled final.

Farlo FC is captained by Manjinder Varya
